Mafikeng Innovation Hub (MIHub)

MIHub is an innovation co-working techno hub in the heart of Mahikeng, the capital city of the North West Province. We focus on unearthing and cultivating innovative solutions that help communities adapt to an ever-changing technological world while improving efficiency and effectiveness across the province.

Business Goals & Objectives
  • Build collaborative, innovative communities that continually engage in sharing resources and transferring knowledge through technology.
  • Introduce, empower, train, and enable entrepreneurs, aspiring entrepreneurs, and tertiary students in the small business sector to succeed in growing and creating sustainable jobs.
  • Use ICT to facilitate, create, develop, grow, and implement innovative ideas that ultimately bear social and economic benefits for South Africa.
  • Add value to existing companies, foster entrepreneurship and technological transfer, generate knowledge-based jobs, and attract innovative companies and institutions.
  • Provide access to appropriate rental space and flexible leases while sharing business services and equipment.

Mission

To empower rural communities by providing accessible, innovative technology solutions that enhance education, productivity, and quality of life.

Vision

To be the leading catalyst for digital transformation in rural areas, fostering sustainable growth and bridging the technology divide.

Objectives

  • Increase digital literacy among rural populations.
  • Provide affordable access to technology tools and training.
  • Collaborate with local stakeholders to identify needs and deliver solutions.
  • Promote sustainable development through innovative tech initiatives.

Values

  • Integrity and transparency in all our actions.
  • Commitment to community empowerment.
  • Innovation and continuous learning.
  • Collaboration and partnership.
